About Us

We are ai.Workforce, a dedicated team of eight professionals with an average of 17 years of IT experience. ai.workforce trains students and professionals in artificial intelligence, full stack development, and embedded systems. We prepare you for a tech-driven future with real-world projects and industry experts. We leverage our diverse backgrounds to provide comprehensive training and mentorship for aspiring IT professionals. By bridging the gap between education and employment, we aim to empower the next generation of IT professionals, ensuring they are equipped with the necessary skills to thrive in a technology-driven environment. Our platform, ai.workforce, serves as an experience center where we cultivate a skilled IT workforce directly from academic institutions, and we focus on identifying and screening potential young talent to meet the evolving demands of the industry too.
